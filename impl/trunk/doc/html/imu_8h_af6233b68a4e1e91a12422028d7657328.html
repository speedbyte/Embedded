Initialize all sensors.

Author
Oliver Breuning (olbrgs00)
Date
2015/05/13

Calls the initialization functions of all mounted sensors. Over the I2C interface the acceleration, electrical compass, gyroscope and barometer sensors are initialized

Parameters
[out]booleanvalue indicating the success of initialization
1: error occured 0: initialization succeeded
